Performance Highlights


  • Awarded 3000 scholarships to scholars from 45 states and 70 countries.

  • Hosted 54 camps.

  • Scholars built 600 projects

  • Trained over 200 instructional leader

  • Launched Kode With Klossy Campus Workshops

  • Season two of our podcast Techsetters premiered. Guests included Amy Jain the CEO & founder of BaubleBar, Anjali Sud CEO of Vimeo, Adam Mosseri head of Instagram, and Bridgit Mendler actress and Ph.D. candidate at MIT, just to name a few!

  • For a third year, we had our Kode With Klossy run club.
